Symptoms by type

  • Breathing problems

    Difficulty breathing, including wheezing or feeling short of breath

  • Pain

    Pain in any part of the body, including headache, chest pain, arm pain, hand pain, knee pain and foot pain

  • Cold and flu symptoms

    Symptoms similar to cold or flu, including high temperature, cough and sore throat

  • Eating and digestion problems

    Symptoms that affect eating and digestion, including teeth grinding, bloating and diarrhoea and vomiting

  • Symptoms in babies and children

    Symptoms that are more common in children, including reflux in babies and irritable hip

  • Skin symptoms

    Any changes to the skin, including rashes, itching and swelling

  • Sight and eye problems

    Problems with your vision, eyes or eyelids

  • Period symptoms

    Symptoms linked to the menstrual cycle, including different periods, post-menopausal bleeding and vaginal dryness

  • Mental health symptoms

    Symptoms linked to mental health, including stress, anxiety, low mood, hallucinations and hearing voices

  • Injuries

    An injury to any part of the body, such as a head injury, sprain or broken bone

If you need help now, and you're not sure what to do

  • Go to 111.nhs.uk or call 111

  • Find your nearest A%E
  • Call 999
